# Security Design Principles

## Layered Security
A layered approach ensures multiple defensive mechanisms are in place, reducing the likelihood of a single point of failure.

## Complete Mediation
All access to critical systems, such as transaction processing and inventory management, is verified to ensure only authorized actions are allowed.

## Least Privilege
Users are granted only the permissions necessary to perform their roles, minimizing the risk of internal misuse or compromise.

## CIA Triad Focus
- Confidentiality: Protect sensitive transaction data  
- Integrity: Ensure accuracy of transactions and inventory
